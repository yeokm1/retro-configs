Xircom CardBus Ethernet PC Card
Introduction

Note: The technical information in this online documentation is valid for the following Xircom products: RealPort2 CardBus Ethernet 10/100, RealPort CardBus Ethernet 10/100, CardBus Ethernet II 10/100, and CardBus Ethernet 10/100.

Xircom CardBus Ethernet PC Card models offer mobile users high-performance 32-bit 10 Mbps and 100 Mbps Ethernet connectivity in a robust integrated PC Card with built-in connectors. This power-saving CardBus adapter features BatterySave™ advanced power management and 3.3-volt operation for longer battery life. It provides the ultimate in performance and reliability when accessing information while in the office.

Installation and management is simpler than ever: The revolutionary RealPort and RealPort2 CardBus Ethernet models provide robust and reliable communications by integrating connectors directly into the card, eliminating the need for cables. Xircom CardBus Ethernet PC Card models are designed for use with Microsoft Windows 2000, Me (Millennium Edition), 98, 95, and NT 4.0.

This online documentation is intended to help you install and troubleshoot your Xircom CardBus Ethernet PC Card.
