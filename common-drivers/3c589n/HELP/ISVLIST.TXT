EtherLink/PC Card Compatibility Guide


 To access the current listing of compatible hardware/software for the 
 EtherLink III LAN PC Card (3C589), call 3Com's CardFacts FAX Service. 
 CardFacts is an automated FAX-back service available 24 hours a day,
 7 days a week.  To access CardFacts from anywhere in the world dial 
 (1) (408) 727-7021 on your touch-tone phone and select Option 1.  
 Request document number 4319 for the PC Card Hardware Compatibility Guide
 and document number 9071 for the Software Compatibility Guide.  The
 documents will be sent to your FAX machine.

 Document number 9999 provides a listing of all currently available
 documents on CardFacts.
